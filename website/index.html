Built for the Modern Minecrafter

Designed from the ground up to be lightweight and completely out of your way. Manage your instances and mods with a beautiful, distraction-free interface.

Native Performance

No heavy web frameworks here. Compiled natively to ensure minimal RAM usage and instant load times, keeping your PC's resources free for the game itself.

Mod Loader Support

Full support for the entire modding ecosystem. Easily install and manage Vanilla, Fabric, Forge, NeoForge, and Quilt instances.

Sleek Design

A dark, glassmorphism-inspired UI that looks right at home on any modern operating system. Minimal clutter, maximum playability.
